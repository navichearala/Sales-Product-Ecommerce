# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analysis — Product & Revenue Intelligence

> A comprehensive data analytics project uncovering revenue trends, top-performing products, customer purchase behavior, and seasonal patterns from e-commerce transaction data.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

E-commerce businesses generate massive volumes of transactional data but often lack clear visibility into what drives revenue, which products underperform, and when peak demand occurs. This project delivers a full sales intelligence analysis to answer these business-critical questions.

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions Answered

- 💰 **Which products and categories generate the most revenue?**
- 📅 **What are the monthly and seasonal sales trends?**
- 📍 **Which geographies drive the highest order volumes?**
- 🛒 **What is the average order value and how does it vary by segment?**
- 🔄 **What is the customer repeat purchase rate?**

---

## 🛠️ Approach & Methodology

1. **Data Cleaning** — Handling nulls, duplicates, incorrect data types, and outliers
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** — Univariate and multivariate analysis of sales, product, and customer dimensions
3. **Revenue Analysis** — Monthly revenue trends, YoY growth rates, top product/category rankings
4. **Customer Segmentation** — RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) for customer value tiers
5. **Visualization** — Bar charts, line plots, heatmaps, and geographic plots
6. **Business Insights** — Actionable recommendations derived from findings

---

## 📈 Sample Insights

- Top 10% of products account for ~65% of total revenue (Pareto principle confirmed)
- Peak sales months: **November & December** (holiday season effect)
- High-value customer segment drives 3x more repeat purchases than average
- Certain product categories show strong weekend sales spikes vs. weekday patterns
